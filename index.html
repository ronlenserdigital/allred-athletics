The Allred Standard

We don't just coach.

Julia and her husband grew up in sport, and both lost seasons to injuries. In college they learned what most athletes never hear: the majority of those injuries were preventable.

So they built Allred around that truth. Julia earned a degree in exercise physiology and works as a certified personal trainer and licensed EMT. Her husband holds a degree in recreational management and is a certified sports evaluator. Every session runs on that foundation: real science, proper form, and gym safety first.

The Progression

From first steps to D1 prep.

Training that meets athletes exactly where they are and carries them all the way through. Each stage builds on the last with the right intensity, the right skills, and the right safety.

  1. 2 to 6

    Little Movers

    Learn to move safely, use equipment the right way, and build a love for the gym early.

  2. 6 to 10

    Foundations

    HIIT, plyometrics, mobility, and functional work with correct form and safety taught first.

  3. 10 to 14

    Development

    Sport focused training, in season versus off season planning, and real nutrition tracking.

  4. 14 to 18

    Elite Prep

    Train like a D1 athlete. Dialed in form, counting macros, sportsmanship, and the mindset to make it.

  5. Adult

    For Life

    Strength, recovery, and moving well for the long haul. Weight loss, toning, or pure healthy living.
